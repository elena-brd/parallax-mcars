Facts about Kia

KIA'S BRANDING IS ABOUT MOVEMENT AND INSPIRATION

Among the many interesting facts about Kia motors, its focus on the European automobile market since 2005 is quite popular.

To enhance its growth in the region, Kia motors hired German car designer Peter Schreyer. This is not just any designer, his famous creations happen to be the Audi TT and he was also affiliated with Volkswagen. The Chief Design Officer of Kia motors Peter Schreyer is responsible for the total overhaul of Kia's stylish range of cars.

KIA HAS A UNIQUE DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Kia cars are known for state-of-art-interior, ergonomic design and the latest technological innovations. Kia motors new design philosophy is “Opposites United.” The company uses concepts of contrast and disruption to create beautiful designs. For instance, the design for Kia Sportage is bold and contemporary, inspired by both modernity and nature.

Random Text Two

KIA IS A TERM FROM SINO-KOREAN LANGUAGE

The term “Kia” originated from the Sino-Korean characters where "Ki" means to come out and "a" stands for East Asia. Combined, Kia is translated to “one, that comes out of the east”.
